About

My name is ToAnh Tran and I am a military spouse and mother. I am attempting to change careers to the tech industry. My eyes have been open to the limitless opportunities that being in the tech industry has to offer. I have two goals: 1- Get a web developer job and 2- Start a non-profit to help military families get off of government assistances and help military spouses find jobs remotely in the tech industry.

If you are a Military Spouse and would like to join a free community dedicated to learning more about web development check out MILSpouseCoders.org
